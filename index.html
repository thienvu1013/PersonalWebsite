Who am I?

My first coding experience was in my first year of engineering. We learned about basic concepts of C++. I found the subject interesting, but didn't pursue it any further as we never really built anything from what we learned.

Fast forward to 2019, I'm sitting on my desk at one of the most well known EPC companies in the country. I was getting frustrated with this one software that we were using. Nothing about the program was intuitive and the design looks like it's from the 90s. I remember the C++ course that I took in my first year and thought to myself "Yeah I can create a better software than this!". That took me to a journey into the tech world. I was amazed at the progression of the tech industry. I find myself staying up at night (voluntary) learning and researching things that I didn't know existed. There was so much to learn and I was excited.

I decided to go back to school and the rest is history.
